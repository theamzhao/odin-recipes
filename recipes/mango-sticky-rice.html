Mango Sticky Rice

This classic Thai dessert is heavenly and scrumptious. Its taste of tropical rice pudding is irresistible - and it's easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Thai sweet rice
  • 1.5 cups water, divided
  • 1 can (13.5oz) coconut milk, divided
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4-5 tsp brown sugar, divided
  • 1-2 ripe mangoes

Instructions

  1. Step 1:

    Soak the rice in 1 cup water in medium pot for 20-30 mins. Do not drain.

  2. Step 2:

    Add 1/2 cup more water, plus 1/2 can of coconut milk, salt, and brown sugar. stir well. Bring to a gentle boil, then partially cover with a lid. Reduce heat to medium-low, or just until you get a gentle simmer.

  3. Step 3:

    Simmer 20-30 mins, or until the coconut water has been absorbed by the rice. Turn off the heat but leave the pot on the burner with the lid on tight. Sit for 5-10 minutes.

  4. Step 4:

    To make the sauce, warm the remaining coconut milk on med-low heat in a small saucepan. Add 3 tbsp brown suggar, stirring to dissolve. Taste-test for sweetness.

  5. Step 5:

    Prepare mangoes by cutting and slicing into bite-sized pieces. Scoop some warm rice into each bowl, and drizzle the sweet coconut sauce on top. Arrange mango slices and finish with more sauce.
